about
I’m a multidisciplinary, community taught artist based in Pittsburgh, working mainly in photography, printmaking, painting, and collage. I enjoy experimenting with materials and blending processes to see what new textures, visuals, and experiences come out.
I’m a senior at the University of Pittsburgh, earning a Bachelor’s in History of Art and Architecture with a minor in Studio Arts. Since 2023, I’ve worked as an Art Educator at the Carnegie Museum of Art, leading programs like camps, tours, workshops, and youth exhibitions that connect diverse audiences with art in inclusive, engaging ways. As a Scholar at Silver Eye Center for Photography, I’ve gained hands-on experience in exhibition development, public writing, fine art printing, and nonprofit gallery work.
Solo Exhibitions /our obsession with/, Inter Gallery, Pittsburgh, PA, 2024 Group Exhibitions PRINTX, North Hills Art Center and Pittsburgh Print Group, Pittsburgh, PA, 2025 Fall Group Show, Bodega Film Lab, Pittsburgh, PA 2022 Pittsburgh Poetry Houses (featured illustrations), Pittsburgh, PA, 2019
